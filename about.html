BrightWay International Academy, G.R.A., Keffi, Nasarawa state exists to provide excellence in all facets of learning, ensuring outstanding professional teaching, classroom and all-round motivation, in order to attain a commendable level of scholastic, academic, and social achievement. Through our curriculum, we aim to develop the full potential of each learner as a citizen of a dynamic world. At the end of the day, we hope to produce a life-long learner who is compassionate, confident, independent, literate, and multi-skilled: a critical learner that has respect for the environment and is able to make a desirable impact on the society. At BrightWay, we value our learners and in return expect them .to uphold the Mission and Vision of the school.
